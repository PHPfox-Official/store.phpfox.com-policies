# Submitting a product to our Store

Submitting a product to our Store

All the products that are added to our Store are first approved by us before it goes public. We also do weekly checks on products to make sure they meet our stores requirements. When your product is denied, don't worry, you can look over this guide to fix any issues.

We will go over each of the sections you see when managing a product.

General

This is where you can add your Products Name, Category and Version. When you adding your products name, try to keep it short and to the point. Give your product a unique name that is also relevant to what it does. Names that overextend and use keywords to explain what it is will not be approved.

Icon

Your Icon is the first thing clients will see and notice when browsing products.

ICONS FOR MODULES & APPS
It needs to be at least 150 pixels in width and height. The icon needs to be a perfect square in order to keep with our stores theme. Create an icon that is relevant to what you are offering. If you are looking for icons to work with you can tons of vector icons at flaticon.

ICONS FOR THEMES
Themes require a screenshot of your theme and needs to be at least 300 pixels in width and 400 pixels in height. When taking a screenshot of your theme, keep these dimensions in mind. Try to end your screenshot where main content ends and try not to include too much extra space on the sides.

ICONS FOR LANGUAGE PACKAGES
Icons for themes need to be 150 pixels in width and height. Try to use a countries flag to identify the language package. This will make it much easier for users browsing the store.

Pricing

Free
Products that are set to free are free to any of our clients.

One time fee
Prices are to be set in USD. Payments are handled by our Store and we accept major credit cards and PayPal. Moxi9 takes care of payment gateway and distribution fees and takes a 20% fee on each purchase. For more information please read over our Developers Program Policy.

Locked Feature
By creating a locked feature you can select which of your products unlock it. When a user purchases/downloads one of your products, which you defined in your unlock list they will then be able to access this product.

Description

This is where you can add your sales pitch and show to the world how awesome your product is. We don't allow HTML on our site, however we do support Markdown. We do not accept products that include images in their description. Please add all your images as screenshots.

Keep in mind our Developers Program Policy and where it is stated...

Products cannot include links to other stores in order to purchase the same product. If you wish to sell your product on another site, that is fine, we simply do accept these links on our site.

Demo

Providing a demo for your product will convert a lot more sales. Make sure to provide the full URL to your demo. If your demo requires login access, provide test accounts in your Description.

Support

For your clients that have purchased and/or downloaded your products they can contact you via email if you wish to provide such support. Email communication is done via our email server and we relay emails to and from your clients. For each of your products you receive a unique email from us. When clients contact you you will receive emails with a unique subject line. Do not alter this subject in order to continue the email conversation with the client.

To enable this option enter your email in the Support section.

Screenshots

We accept 4 screenshots per product. There are no set limits as to the size of your images, however we resize images to a max of 300 pixels width and height to create thumbnails for your products page.

As there are no set limits to the size, if your image did not work, try another image.

To order your images, you can simply drag them into the position you want. Once you have done that make sure to hit Save.

Product Files

This is where you can add your ZIP files and this is the ZIP file clients will get once they are able to download your product.

Installs

This section list all the clients that have purchased/downloaded your product.
